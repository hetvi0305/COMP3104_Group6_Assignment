George Brown College (GBC) is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 
It offers a wide range of diploma, degree, and certificate programs in fields such 
as computer technology, business, health sciences, and design.

GBC emphasizes applied learning, where students gain both theoretical knowledge 
and practical, hands-on experience. The college also provides opportunities to 
connect with industry partners, which helps students build professional skills 
and prepare for the workplace.
